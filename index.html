About
Me

A Creative and Detail-oriented UI/UX designer with Hands-on Experience in Designing Responsive, user-centric interfaces for web, mobile, and VR platforms. I specialize in UX research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing, using tools like Figma, Adobe XD, and Framer. With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.js, I bridge the gap between design and development to create intuitive digital experiences. From healthcare apps to e-commerce platforms and VR interfaces, my focus is always on solving real user problems through thoughtful, accessible, and engaging design.
