Career
Story

My journey as a UI/UX designer began with a passion for crafting digital experiences that feel intuitive, inclusive, and visually engaging. During my internships at Twin Reality Technologies and Infotact Solutions, I worked on real-world design challenges—ranging from VR user interfaces to healthcare and booking platforms—where I learned the importance of design thinking, accessibility, and seamless user flows.

I also explored front-end development at Unified Mentor, where I translated design into responsive layouts using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.js, bridging the gap between UI and code. These experiences sharpened my skills in user research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing.

Today, I continue to push boundaries by designing digital products that solve real problems and improve lives—one interface at a time.
