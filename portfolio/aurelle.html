Project Overview

This project involved designing a responsive, user-centric website for a young gynecologist looking to expand her clinic's online presence beyond social media. The goal was to create a trustworthy, informative platform that allows patients to access key services, book appointments seamlessly, and easily reach the clinic despite its challenging location. The website needed to reflect professionalism while remaining approachable, offering both utility and reassurance to diverse patient demographics.

Process

The project began with a detailed client interview to understand her goals, services, and user pain points. These insights laid the foundation for a broader UX research phase that included competitive analysis and user interviews with women aged 25–65. The research aimed to validate assumptions and uncover opportunities to improve the overall patient experience.

Using FigJam, affinity mapping was conducted to synthesize key findings. User personas were developed to represent three core patient types: a working mother, a university student, and a retired woman. Their goals and frustrations informed every design decision.

Key research insights included:

  • The importance of offering multiple booking options (calls, messages, online platform).
  • A need for concise, trustworthy health information in a professional yet friendly tone.
  • Strong concern around parking and clinic accessibility.
  • Preference for digital confirmations and reminders.

With these findings, we prioritized key features and structured the website's sitemap around high-impact user journeys—such as booking a visit, locating parking, and reading about available services.

Wireframes were built for both mobile and desktop, focusing on six essential pages: Homepage, About, Services, Pregnancy, Directions, and Book a Visit. A clean UI component library was developed, setting typography, color schemes, and reusable elements. Using Figma, I created a high-fidelity prototype with interactive animations, a sticky navigation bar, and a responsive accordion for FAQs.

Challenges

One of the primary challenges was ensuring that the website remained lightweight while maintaining high performance under heavy traffic. Optimizing loading times and ensuring a smooth user experience across all devices required detailed attention to coding practices and image compression. Another challenge was aligning the interactive elements, such as animations and hover effects, with the website's overall minimalistic design without overwhelming users.

Result

The final product was a fast, responsive, and visually compelling website that met the client’s needs and exceeded performance expectations. The site’s dynamic design and easy navigation contributed to a significant increase in user engagement, while the seamless integration of Webflow made ongoing content management simple for the client. The website was well-received and successfully supported the company’s online growth and outreach efforts.
